Our Story

  • “A while after we became friends, I knew that I had feelings for her, but I didn’t think it would be a good idea to address it and so we stayed friends for a really long time.”

  • “We bonded over our shared love for music and black coffee. Back when exchanging music over Bluetooth was a thing, I’d share my playlists with him in exchange for Frooti packs.

    But never in a million years did I think that I’d end up marrying him. He’s shown me what being loved feels like- be it picking me up after work with a sandwich or buying me an old rotary phone that I’d been eyeing for months or simply even waiting with me for delayed trains at the railway station”.

  • “From the day we became friends almost a decade ago till now, there hasn’t been a dull moment with her around. She makes me laugh, and that’s what I love the most about her. She’s everything I could ask for in a person. She’s my best friend first and everything else next”.

  • “Over the years, we’ve moved cities; even continents. Changed jobs, changed numbers. Grown seas apart in ideologies and even closer, in love. The time-worn picture of ours, in my wallet is a testament to this”.

As we celebrate our wedding, we’d love for all of you to be part of our joy, even if not in person. We wish you and your loved ones happiness and health till things return to normal and we meet again!

- Arjun and Gaachu
